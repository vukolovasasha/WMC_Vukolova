Cats have always inspired people with their mystery and playful spirit. From the strange drawings in medieval manuscripts to the graceful companions we know today, they remind us that beauty can be both curious and unique.

Throughout history, cats have carried rich cultural meanings. In medieval art, they were often portrayed as mysterious or even unsettling creatures, reflecting society’s mixed feelings toward them. Today, these same animals are cherished as symbols of comfort, independence, and creativity — a reminder of how perspectives evolve over time.
